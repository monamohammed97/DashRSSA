President message

Arwa Badeeb MBBS D.ABR

Assistant Professor of Radiology

King Abdulaziz University Hospital (Jeddah)

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to our new website, which we envision to be a valuable resource for radiology professionals in Saudi Arabia, as well as for those outside the country to be updated on the many activities that we organize for our colleagues throughout the year.

Here you will also find useful information on becoming a member of the society and its numerous benefits, as well as how you can support our events through partnership and sponsorships. This is only one step among many we hope to take as we hope to enhance the services of the organization and help contribute to the advancement of radiology as a profession into the future.

Warm regards,

Nuha Abdullah

Khoumayies MD, MBA IHM
